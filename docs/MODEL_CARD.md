# Model Card

## Status

No production model exists. Phase 0 audited inputs and pre-specified future comparisons only.

## Intended use

Future models may prioritize imidazolium NHC precursor candidates for additional electronic-energy calculations. They are not predictors of experimental synthesis success, solution chemistry, complete 298.15 K Gibbs free energy, or Cu(111) binding.

## Current evidence

Historical legacy evidence shows strong raw xTB/DFT rank correlation on 71 labels and a free affine slope different from 1. Those numbers are audit context, not new-repository validation and do not promote any model.

## Restrictions

- lower electronic energy ranks better;
- no claim of frequency-confirmed minima for skipped-Hessian labels;
- no broad same-run xTB descriptor model by default;
- no full-pool production score before Phase 4 promotion;
- no previously revealed holdout may be called a new blind test.

This card will be replaced with a versioned model-specific card after Phase 4, including dataset/model hashes, validation protocols, uncertainty limitations, applicability domain, failure modes, and promotion decision.
